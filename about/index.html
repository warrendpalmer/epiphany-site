About Our Family

Epiphany Lawn & Garden is a family‑owned and family‑run landscaping business based in Richardson, Texas. We believe in honest work, dependable service, and the simple pleasures of a well‑kept yard. Our team cares for lawns, irrigation systems, trees, drainage, and fertilizing needs across North Texas as if they were our own.

When you invite us to your property, you’re welcoming our family. We show up on time, communicate clearly, and leave your yard better than we found it. It’s a partnership we hope lasts for seasons to come.
